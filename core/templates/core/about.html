About Strantell

Business intelligence built from operational reality.

Strantell LLC is a Phoenix-based Business Intelligence and Operations Analytics consulting firm helping operations-driven businesses turn scattered reporting, underused data, and unclear KPI tracking into structured analytical systems leaders can trust.

Request an Assessment View Services

Why Strantell Exists

Reporting gets harder as operations get larger.

Strantell was founded to serve organizations where operational complexity has grown faster than reporting capability. In industrial environments, reporting and KPI tracking can quickly become difficult to manage. Spreadsheets multiply, definitions become inconsistent, and leaders may lose visibility into performance, bottlenecks, and emerging risks.

Strantell exists to help businesses make the transition into practical, reliable business intelligence without losing sight of how the operation actually works.

Operating Principle

Strantell begins on the floor, not at the computer.

Useful analytics starts with operational understanding. Whenever practical, Strantell analysts step onto the floor, observe workflows directly, speak with the people involved, and identify the decisions leaders need to make with greater confidence.

Built for Operations

Focused on businesses where real work happens in the field, lab, shop, or production environment.

Strantell serves operations-driven organizations across the Phoenix metro area, especially businesses that make, move, repair, test, or build things.
