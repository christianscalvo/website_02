What to Send

Start with the operational problem, not the software.

You do not need a perfect dataset or a fully defined dashboard request. A useful first message simply explains what reporting problem you are trying to solve, where the data currently lives, and what decisions would be easier with better visibility.

Helpful details to include

  • What kind of operation you run or manage.
  • What reports, spreadsheets, dashboards, or systems you currently use.
  • What metrics are hard to track consistently.
  • Where the data lives: Excel, Power BI, SQL, Snowflake, ERP, LIMS, or another system.
  • What decisions you wish leadership could make faster.
